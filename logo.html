Brand Usage and Guidelines

Before downloading the logos, please be sure to follow the basic rules below.

Download the Logos

Correct Usage

Please follow the instructions, and if you have a need that isn't met here, feel free to get in touch at logos@toledofirst.com.

Title Font: Comfortaa
Subtitle Font: Syncopate

Transparency and Text

Use the white logo to contrast and be sure the "dot" in the location pin is transparent. If space allows, use the subtitle "Assembly of God."

Color

The location pin should always match the title text color. Ensure your colors contrast with the background so the logo stands out.

Incorrect Usage

Please don't modify the logos or use them in a confusing way.

Wrong Fonts or Old Logos

Don't use incorrect font cobinations or old logos. Correct fonts are: 

Title Font: Comfortaa
Subtitle Font: Syncopate

Wrong Pin

Our location pin logo is a "teardrop" style. Do not substitue other location pin styles other than the approved custom version.
