what can i expect?

How long is a Toledo First service?

In total, a Toledo First church service is about 90 minutes in length. Services begin with the Toldo First worship band leading the church in music - song lyrics are projected onto the screens so you can sing along and/or engage with worship however you feel most comfortable. After the music portion of the service is complete, one of our pastors or missionaries will come out to share an encouraging and hope-filled message about Jesus.

What's the culture like at Toledo First?

Sunday’s at Toledo First are exciting, casual, and relaxed. Come as you are and expect to feel welcomed as our guest.

  • What about my kids?
  • What about youth/teens?

We believe that kids should have a blast at church every single week - and at Toledo First SHINE Kids, we make this a priority. The other thing we make a priority is your children’s safety. Because of that, we have a detailed check-in process for our Toledo First SHINE Kids program the first time that you visit. You’ll want to leave yourself an extra ten minutes to get signed in for the Toledo First SHINE Kids experience. Toledo First SHINE Kids is offered at every Toledo First service for kids ages infant through Grade 6.
